Dear RA Alums
Resident Assistants are the most important peer support for students in college, especially at NYU. So, we wanted to find a way to connect you, an RA Alum, back to your NYU roots and connect you to current RAs who are going through the same experiences you did when you were a student and so they can learn from your journey.
Therefore, we are excited to unveil our new website: The RA-Alumni Network!
This website will allow you to...
Browse Through Memories Take a walk down memory lane and see pictures other Alum have submitted from their time as an RA! Plus, you can also add your own memories to our website!! Get Involved in RA-Alum Events & Opportunities Check out events where you can connect with current RAs whether that be at the monthly brunches, Alumni Weekend at NYU, or facilitate training sessions for RAs.
Post Internship Opportunities Looking for an internship can be intimidating! Help current NYU RAs by making the process a little easier. If you work at a company that offers internships, you can post them on this website! Become a Mentor Connect with a current RA that is interested in going into the same career field as you. You can share advice, experiences, and memories of how being an RA has helped your current career!!
See Alumni Accomplishments Whether it be writing a book, starting a company, starring in a film/broadway show, or receiving a prestigious award in your career field, see what RA Alum have accomplished since their time at the square! Check out RA's Favorite Books Since 2010, Tom Ellett, the Sr. AVP of Student Affairs has read every single RAs favorite book! See which book is the most popular and you can even add your own book should it not be on the list!
